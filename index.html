About

Hey everyone, thanks for stopping by. I hope you find some inspiration while you are here. I joined the arts back in high school to develop connections with people, build relationships with the masses, and show everyone that they can think differently if they try. I enjoy performing in all realms of the arts whether it be dance, music, film, comedy, drama, or even painting...even though I am not the best painter. Then again, that's part of the fun. As I grow as an artist, so does my ability to connect with others. Fun fact: I'm an avid chess player. Feel free to contact me with any questions, comments, or award winning projects you might have coming up. See you around.
